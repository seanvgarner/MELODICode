Getting Started!

X

Melodicode is a musical step sequencer. The sequencer is a large grid of cells. Each of the lighter colored cells are notes. Each row of the note cells correspond to a note in the pentatonic scale so most notes sound good together! The darker cells on the bottom of the grid are simple drum kit sounds: Kick, Snare, Clap and Hi Hat. Click on any of the cells (or click and drag on the note cells) to activate them. Once you have a melody sequence mapped out, hit play and listen to the sequence in a loop. Thats it! You made a melody! Press Stop at anytime to stop the loop.


Some useful buttons and controls:


  • Tempo: Slide this slider right or left to increase/decrease the tempo (speed) of the loop.
  • Clear: Workspace getting cluttered? click this button to clear the grid completely and start with a blank slate.
  • Preset Loops: If you are having trouble getting started or you need some inspiration, click on one of these demo presets and the grid will populate with a premade melody that you can play. Tinker with them as you like!

If all else fails, just click around a bit and hit play, you'd be surprised at what you might accidentally create.
